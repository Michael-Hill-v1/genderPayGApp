The Calculation:

A list is made of all female full-pay relevant employees.
This list is is put in order from highest to lowest hourly pay.
The hourly pay of the woman in the middle of the list is the median hourly pay for women.

A list is made of all male full-pay relevant employees.
This list is is put in order from highest to lowest hourly pay.
The hourly pay of the man in the middle of the list is the median hourly pay for men.

The median hourly pay for women is subtracted from the median hourly pay for men.
The result is divided by the median hourly pay for men, then multiplied by 100.
This number is the median gender pay gap in hourly pay.
